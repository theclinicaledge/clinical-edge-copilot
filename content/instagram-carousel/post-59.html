Slide 04 — The Clue
The Clue
04 / 08
A-Fib
No organized P waves.
Disorganized atrial activity. The baseline is fibrillating — no true P-wave before each QRS.
PACs
PACs interrupt an organized rhythm.
A P-wave appears before the early QRS — it may look slightly different, but it's there. The underlying sinus rhythm continues.
