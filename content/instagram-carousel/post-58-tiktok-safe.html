What this could be

Possible concerns and targeted assessments — structured for the bedside.

Clinical Edge
COPILOT
Structured clinical reasoning — confirm with your assessment
URGENCY: HIGH
What this could be
A sudden BP drop with compensatory tachycardia after a stable period points toward an acute hemodynamic shift worth investigating at the bedside.
What to assess next
›Mental status — confusion, agitation, or unusual quietness
›Skin perfusion — mottling, pallor, temperature, capillary refill
›Work of breathing — tachypnea or accessory muscle use
›What changed in the last hour — medication, fluid, new complaint
›Volume status — dry mucous membranes, flat neck veins, recent output
