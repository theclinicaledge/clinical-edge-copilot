Slide 06 — What To Focus On
What to focus on
06 / 08

After the PRN, pause.

01

What actually improved?

Pain score, appearance, effort. Be specific.

02

What didn’t?

Vitals trend, work of breathing, skin, mental status.

03

What’s still trending wrong?

Compare to baseline. One hour ago. Four hours ago.

04

Notify provider if the picture is changing

Don’t wait for it to be obvious. Early is always better.
