Slide 04 — What Matters
What Matters
04 / 08

Oxygen didn’t fix the problem.

It only increased available oxygen

The delivery went up. The reason for the drop did not change.

It didn’t address why they were desatting

Sat is a symptom. Something caused that symptom.

The underlying issue is still there

Better numbers can mask a worsening patient.

Slide 06 — Nursing Action
Nursing Action
06 / 08

What you do next.

01

Reassess respiratory effort and pattern

Rate, depth, accessory muscle use. Document what you see.

02

Listen to lung sounds

Crackles, wheeze, reduced entry. Note any change from earlier.

03

Compare to earlier baseline

Is this worse than an hour ago? Two hours? Trend matters.

04

Notify provider if condition is changing

Don’t wait for the sat to drop again. Act on the trend.
