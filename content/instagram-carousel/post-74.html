Slide 03 — The Trick
The ABG Trick
03 / 05
Here’s the trick.
CO₂ = Respiratory
CO₂ moves opposite the pH
↑ CO₂ → ↓ pH
↓ CO₂ → ↑ pH
HCO₃⁻ = Metabolic
HCO₃ moves with the pH
↑ HCO₃ → ↑ pH
↓ HCO₃ → ↓ pH
CO₂ = opposite  ·  HCO₃ = same
