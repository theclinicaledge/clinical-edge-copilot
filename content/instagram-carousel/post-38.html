What this could be

Neurotoxicity can develop even when the antibiotic appears to be working.

  • medication-related neurotoxicity
  • renal clearance issue
  • delirium from infection or hospitalization
  • metabolic shift contributing to mentation change

What to assess next

The assessment goes beyond the infection.

  • mental status compared to baseline
  • renal trend and recent creatinine
  • timing of cefepime doses vs symptoms
  • tremor, twitching, myoclonus, seizure activity
  • other meds affecting mentation

Where this may be heading

01

Reversible medication effect

Worsening delirium

03

Seizure risk depending on severity
