Slide 07 — What I'd Do Right Now
Copilot

What I’d do right now

01

Reassess before any more fluids

The BP improved. That doesn’t mean more volume is the answer now.

02

Pause and reevaluate trajectory

What direction is the patient moving? Not just where they are right now.

03

Escalate if respiratory status is changing

Wetter lungs + increasing O₂ need = a trend worth communicating.

04

Communicate both sides clearly

BP improved. Breathing worsening. Both matter. Report both.

Slide 08 — Copilot Note
Copilot Note
08 / 09

Improvement in one system can stress another.

Always reassess the full picture after interventions.

The goal is not just to move the number. It’s to move the patient in the right direction.

After any significant intervention, ask: what improved, what changed, and what might now be at risk.
